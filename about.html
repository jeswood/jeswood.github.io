Songs To Dance To is a free weekly newsletter featuring 10 handpicked songs... to dance to.

So, what is dance music?

Fuck if I know.

Dance music is both an adjective and a genre (which is just a bastardized version of an adjective), and like all genres, it's impossible to truly define.

But for S2D2 the definition is simple: it's music that makes you want to move, whether it's at a club, in the car, or at work. It's mostly electronic but it doesn't have to be.
